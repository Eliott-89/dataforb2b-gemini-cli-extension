# DataForB2B — B2B People & Company Search

You have access to the DataForB2B API, a live B2B data platform with 70+ filters sourced across 60+ public sources. Use these tools whenever the user wants to find leads, prospect, enrich contacts, or research companies.

## Available tools

### `search_natural_language`
Search for people or companies using a natural language query.

### `enrich_profile`
Enrich a LinkedIn profile URL with verified contact data.

### `enrich_company`
Get detailed company data from a domain or LinkedIn company URL.

### `search_lookalike`
Find companies or people similar to a given profile.

### `find_email`
Find a verified professional email address for a specific person.
